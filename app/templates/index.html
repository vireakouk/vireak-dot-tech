Mentorship

I offer mentorship and knowledge sharings to aspiring IT & Network engineers and tech industry professionals who are committed to learn and improve oneselves.

My knowledge is limited what I have learnt and experienced, so I won't say I have the absolute best knowledge and that what I know are always right. But I believe that we don't have to know everything or become enlightened to share knowledge to others. I know some who learnt very deep and highly advanced stuff, but rarely use their knowledge for anything meaningful. To me, it's better to know 10 things and share 10 things, instead of knowing 1000 things and share only 1 thing.

I share everything that I have learnt including intermediate and advanced IP network design, management & leadership practices, product development, sales & digital communication strategies, tips and tricks or just general career advices. So if you want to follow along with me or just want to compliment your knowledge with another perspective, don't hesitate to drop me a message and start learning.

This mentorship is open only to individuals who cannot afford proper trainings. If you or your organizations can afford, please consider my training services.
